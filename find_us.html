 Plane

The next Airport to Darmstadt is Frankfurt Airport, 30Km from Darmstadt City.
Frankfurt Airport
From here you can take the "Airliner" shuttle transport to Darmstadt Hauptbahnhof. When you arrived in Darmstadt, you can easily take a Taxi or the next Bus to "Darmstadt Schloß". From here, the Fraunhofer Institut is only 200 meter away. You just have to walk the way up, which you can find on the right of the Darmstadium.


 Car

Darmstadt got a very good connection to the motorways A5 and A67. When you have reached Darmstadt, easily follow the Signs to "Darmstadtium".
From here, the Fraunhofer Institut is only 200 meter away. You just have to walk the way up, which you can find on the right of the Darmstadium.
