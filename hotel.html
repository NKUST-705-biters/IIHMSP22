Hotel Information

 

 

 

 

 

Friendship Palace Hotel was built in 1954. It is a 4-star hotel, renowned for its architectural style, combining both Chinese and Western flavors. As a landmark building of Harbin city, the hotel in situated in the central area of Youyi Road, which is the best place to view riverside scenery.

Adjacent to most of the tourist destinations, the hotel enjoys convenience for conferences, business, tourism, and shopping. There are more than 200 modern rooms, conference halls, and restaurants in the hotel. The rooms are clear, bright and elegant in their simplicity. The hotel staff go out of their way to assist you and provide you with professional and efficient service. After staying at the Friendship Palace Hotel, Harbin, you will understand why China's northeast is known for its hospitality.

Opened in 1954, decorated in 2005.
