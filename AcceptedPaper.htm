Please notice that the deadline for final camera-ready paper is 15th, September, 2021, and this deadline cannot be extended. Later submission will not be included into the proceedings of IIHMSP 2021. Please carefully follow the steps below to prepare the camera-ready paper.

STEP 1

Format your paper according to the LNCS template and build the PDF file of your manuscript:
http://www.springer.com/series/11156
(LaTeX) llncs2e.zip and typeinst.zip, or
(MS-Word 2007) splnproc1110.zip

Paper length:
The length of Paper is limited to 8-10 LNCS pages, including all text, figures, references and appendices.

STEP 2

Complete the attached consent to publish form
Note that (1) we do not accept digital signatures, so please make sure that all forms have been signed in ink; (2) one author may sign on behalf of all the authors of a particular paper; (3) scan the completed copyright form in electronic format (in PDF) and rename it to be IIHMSP2021_XXX_cr.pdf, where XXX is your paper ID.

STEP 3

Create a folder named IIHMSP2021_XXX, and place the following files in the folder. Notice that you should include all the source files, e.g., if you are using Latex, then you should include LaTeX2e files for the text and PS/EPS or PDF/JPG files for all figures, as well as the Final DVI file.
3.1 the PDF file, and named it as IIHMSP2021_XXX.pdf (from Step 1)
3.2 the source file, and named it as IIHMSP2021_XXX.tex or IIHMSP2021_XXX.doc (latex files or word file (from Step 1)
3.3 the consent to publish (from Step 2)

STEP 4

Zip up the folder from Step 3 and call it IIHMSP2021_XXX.zip

STEP 5

Submit the zip file IIHMSP2021_XXX.zip (from Step 4) with subject "Camera Ready Submission: IIHMSP2021" to the IIHMSP office iihmsp21@bit.nkust.edu.tw
