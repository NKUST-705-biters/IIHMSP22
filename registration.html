Registration

All attendees for IIHMSP 2021/ FITAT 2021 are kindly requested to register. Related forms and supporting documents must be received with payment in US$ (US Dollar) or NT$ (NT Dollar) no later than September 15, 2021. Please note that at least one author per paper must register before September 15, 2021. Otherwise the paper will not be included in the IIHMSP 2021/ FITAT 2021 technical program and proceedings. Please also note that the page length limit is 6-88-10 pages. Extra pages will incur extra page charges. Each paper is allowed to have maximum 2 extra pages with raising US$ 100 or NT$2,900 registration fee per extra page.
The registration fee includes conference proceedings, published by Conference Publishing Services, technical sessions, welcome reception, coffee breaks, lunches, and a banquet. An official receipt for the payment will be offered to the registrant on the conference site.
