Renewed Procedure for Final Submission

 

 

Dear Contributing Author,

 

Thank you for your contributing works to IIHMSP-2008.

 

We would like to report to you that there are some changes in the submission procedure for the camera-ready copy and the copyright release form for IIHMSP-2008.

 

All papers included in the proceedings of IIHMSP-2008 will be also included in the IEEE Xplore digital library (the IEL). To ensure the availability and the compatibility of your camera-ready copy, the Conference Publishing Services (CPS) of IEEE Computer Society had prepared a renewed online submission system and enforces our authors to conduct the final submission via it.

 

Please refer to the following renewed information regarding the final submission and registration:

 

1.      Due date for camera-ready copy and copyright release form: May 7, 2008.

Please refer to http://www.ieeeconfpublishing.org/cpir/AuthorKit.asp?Community=CPS&Facility=CPS_Aug&ERoom=IIHMSP+2008 for further details.

2.      Due date for conference registration: April 30, 2008.

Please refer to http://iihmsp2008.hit.edu.cn or http://bit.kuas.edu.tw/~iihmsp08 for further details.

 

 

The Author Kit Notification, from Ms. Stephanie Kawada - the Production Editor of CPS, is enclosed for your reference.

 

We deeply appreciate your kind cooperation and sincerely apologize for any inconvenience it may cause to you.

Please feel free to let us know (iihmsp08@bit.kuas.edu.tw) for any need of technical assistance regarding the submission of your camera-ready copies and copyright release forms.
