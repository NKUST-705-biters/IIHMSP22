News of IIH-MSP-2021

Conference Information

2021-10-27
Dear all contributors:

Thank you for your contribution to IIH-MSP 2021. IIH-MSP 2021 will be held on Oct 29-31. Please refer to the attached guidebook including the conference program.
In view of the COVID-19 pandemic, IIH-MSP 2021 will be held fully online via the Tencent VooV Meeting platform. Please refer to https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1054/35553 for more information about the usage of Tencent VooV Meeting system. It is highly recommended to install VooV Meeting Application beforehand.
In order to ensure transition smoothly between presentations, we need all presentations in the online system early in advance. Any videos inside the presentation should be provided also as separate video files. Please email your presentation materials including PowerPoint slides and video files (if any) to iihmsp21@bit.nkust.edu.tw with the IIH-MSP 2021 paper ID as Email subject. by 28 Oct. 17:00 TST (GMT+8).

2021-09-05
The Acceptance List is Announced
The review process is finished with the assistance from the program committee members. The acceptance lists can be found at Here. Please contact us via iihmsp21@bit.nkust.edu.tw if you have any question.

The template for IIHMSP-2021 follows the same template for the book series: (LaTeX) llncs2e.zip and typeinst.zip, and (MS-Word 2007) splnproc1110.zip.
The paper length is 8-10 pages for the regular paper. Each paper is allowed to have maximum 2 extra pages with raising USD 100 registration fee per extra page.
