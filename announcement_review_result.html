For those authors who have their papers accepted for oral presentation in IIH-MSP 2017, you are kindly reminded with the following notes:

  • It is required that, for each accepted paper, at least one of the authors registers and attends the conference.
  • Please follow EXACTLY the template from Springer (http://www.springer.com/series/8767) in the preparation of your camera-ready copies and copyright release forms. Please submit your camera-ready copies and copyright release forms by May 1, 2017.
  • As a prerequisite of having your papers included in the proceedings, conference registration is due by May 1, 2017. Please refer to http://bit.kuas.edu.tw/~iihmsp17 for detailed information regarding the conference registration.

 

Please feel free to let us know (iihmsp17@bit.kuas.edu.tw) for any need of technical assistance regarding the preparation of your camera-ready copies and copyright release forms.

 

Finally, we would like to express our appreciation to all your contribution, and look forward to meeting you all in Matsue in this August.
